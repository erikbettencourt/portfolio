AI-Powered Research: Faster Insights, Smarter Decisions

We used AI-driven analysis to make sense of large amounts of qualitative and quantitative research quickly and accurately. Instead of manually sifting through interviews and survey responses, AI helped us surface key trends, spot inconsistencies, and uncover patterns that might have been missed otherwise.

Turning Conversations Into Actionable Insights

Our research with Aledade's Practice Transformation Specialists (PTS) gave us a deeper understanding of how practices use performance data. Using AI-powered transcription and topic modeling, we analyzed conversations across multiple markets to identify common pain points and opportunities.

What AI helped us uncover:

  • Goal setting isn't one-size-fits-all—it's a collaborative, evolving process that varies by practice size, specialty, and experience with value-based care.
  • Real world data inconsistency affected trust metrics. It eroded the Aledade app showed conflicting metrics, it eroded trust. AI helped us flag inconsistencies across data sources to ensure reliability.
  • Personalization helped us categorize practices based on behavior and needs, making it easier to design tailored solutions.

Turning Beta Feedback Into Actionable Insights

Our research with Aledade's Practice Transformation Specialists (PTS) gave us a deeper understanding of how practices use performance data. Using AI-powered transcription and topic modeling, we analyzed conversations across multiple markets to identify common pain points and opportunities.

What practices told us (and what AI confirmed):

  • Accuracy is everything. Practices depend on real-time, reliable data to track performance. AI-driven validation helped us ensure data integrity.
  • Simplicity matters. Users wanted clickable metrics leading to deeper insights and time-range selectors for tracking trends over time.
  • Benchmarking motivates action. Peer comparisons and color-coded feedback made it easier to spot progress and problem areas at a glance.

From Pain Points to Opportunities: Mapping the User Journey

Research is only valuable if it drives action. We mapped pain points across the entire performance journey—both for practices and Aledade's teams—to pinpoint friction and high-impact opportunities. Then, we prioritized solutions based on feasibility and impact.

To scale the beta from 60 to 1,500 practices, we focused on three high-impact UX improvements informed by our research. These enhancements streamlined scorecard usability and seamlessly integrated with existing workflows led by practice transformation specialists.

UX Showcase: Goal Configuration Explorations
